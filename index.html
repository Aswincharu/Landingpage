VARIETIES

Alphonsa

The Alphonso mango, known for its rich, creamy texture and sweet, fragrant flavor, is a premium variety from India, often dubbed the "king of mangoes".

Malawi

The Malawi mango, renowned for its vibrant flavor and juicy flesh, is a popular variety cultivated in the warm climates of Malawi.

Hapus

The Hapus mango is celebrated for its sweet, rich flavor and smooth texture, making it a highly sought-after variety from India.

Neelam

The Neelam mango, prized for its aromatic scent and distinctive sweet taste, is a late-season variety widely grown in India.

Ataulfo

The Ataulfo mango, also known as the Honey mango, is cherished for its creamy texture, sweet flavor, and vibrant yellow skin.

Totapuri

The Totapuri mango, easily recognized by its elongated shape and greenish-yellow skin, is known for its tangy taste and firm, fibrous flesh.
